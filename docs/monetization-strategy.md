# Phase I: Monetization and Revenue Systems

## Overview
Phase I scales the operating platform to handle commercialization—which implies growing beyond AdSense. The focus shifts to organizing mid-tier sponsorship growth, mapping brand-fit scores, and tracking affiliate revenue, all while ensuring governance (Phase G) and team workflows (Phase H) still process the core video production.

## Features
- **Monetization Dashboard**: High-level estimates giving the Producer visibility into the AdSense baselines vs Sponsor deals.
- **Sponsor CRM**: A simple pipeline tracker. Deals attach back to projects so you can see "Sponsor A paid $X for Video B". We also track a "Brand Fit Score".
- **Affiliate Hub**: A centralized vault for tracking affiliate links (Amazon, software, etc.), managing commission expectations, and distributing them rapidly into Metadata.
- **Project Monetization Panel**: Gives Editors and Producers the ability to tag a video as "Early Access for Members," link corresponding deals, and inspect its revenue breakdown over time.

## Why it matters
Scaling a media business is not just about producing more videos (which can lead to burnout or quality drops), but by increasing the yield on the videos that are produced. Tracking *where* revenue comes from gives operations the data needed to decide which series are worth producing.
