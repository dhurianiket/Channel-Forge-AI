# Channel Ops Dashboard

## Objective
The `/channel-ops` view provides a snapshot required by the Producer / EIC to decide what to greenlight next.

## Core Metrics Addressed
1. **Backlog Depth:** Shows if ideas are outpacing output or vice versa.
2. **Publish Consistency:** Over 30 days.
3. **Retention Health:** Tracks AVD alongside 30-sec and 50% marks to assess if narrative beats are landing.

This view informs the decision logic:
- Which series deserves more output?
- Are we losing viewers early (thumbnail/hook mismatch)?
- Is the team producing fast enough for the backlog?
